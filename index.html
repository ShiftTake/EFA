FAQ

Do users have to upload files?

No. MVP supports YouTube and Google Drive links so users don’t download/re-upload huge files.

What powers the captions?

Whisper-grade transcription (multi-language). This frontend is wired for easy backend swap (Groq/Whisper, etc.).

How do you find “viral moments”?

We score energy (volume spikes), laughter detection, and hook sentences in the first seconds.

Is it mobile friendly?

Yes. This landing page is responsive and the dashboard flow can be built mobile-first.
